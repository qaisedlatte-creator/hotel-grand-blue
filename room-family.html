Family Room

"Space to breathe. A retreat that remembers what togetherness should feel like."

The Family Room at Grand Blue Star is designed for those who travel together but value their own space. A generous footprint hosts a separated sleeping area alongside a central living space, giving each guest room to be — whether that means a long breakfast, a quiet afternoon, or simply the ease of not being on top of each other. A scenic balcony extends the room outward, where Mangaluru's air carries with it the faint salt of the coast.
